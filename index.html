1. WEDDING PACKAGES
Great and memorable day for two people united. Our goal is to provide a delicious, natural options and best quality service for the celebration of couple's marriage. We offer different themes for this event; the RUSTIC THEME and the FILIPINO WEDDING THEME.

The simple and grounded feel, combined with light pastel colors, give the rustic wedding a calmer and intimate feel.

If you want your wedding to have a more natural and simple feel, then a rustic wedding theme is for you. Guests can wear simple light-fabric formal wear and dresses. It is best to enjoy a rustic wedding in an outdoor venue, like a large garden or field. To match the natural feel of a rustic themed wedding, your wedding food catering cuisine must have a good blend of greens and fresh meat.

Filipino-style wedding is similar to a traditional wedding, but with a more local flavour for its wedding food catering.

With the Filipino theme in mind, it would be best to wear a barong Tagalog for the male guests and Baro’t Saya for the female guests. For complete effect, you can have the groom and bride (and even their parents) be brought to the venue by way of kalesa. For decorations, a selection of beautiful Filipino flowers such as Sampaguita and gumamela, and some abaca and bamboo banners and arches would definitely give your wedding that authentic Filipino feel.
